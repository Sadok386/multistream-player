Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

MultiStream is an Android application that allows you to watch multiple live streams (Twitch, YouTube, Kick) simultaneously. This policy explains how your data is handled.

Data we do not collect

MultiStream does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on a server. We have no servers, no databases, no analytics services, and no advertising identifiers. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or account information
  • Your location
  • Your device identifiers
  • Usage or analytics data

Data stored locally

The application saves your stream presets (URL combinations) and your favorites (saved streams) locally on your device via Android DataStore. This data never leaves your device and is automatically deleted when you uninstall the application.

Third-party embedded content

MultiStream displays streams via the official embedded players of third-party platforms. These platforms may set cookies and collect data in accordance with their own policies:

  • YouTube (Google) — Google Privacy Policy
  • Twitch (Amazon) — Twitch Privacy Notice
  • Kick — Kick Privacy Policy

MultiStream has no control over and does not access the data collected by these platforms.

Security

Local data is protected by Android’s built-in security mechanisms (application sandboxing). Communications with third-party platforms use HTTPS.

Children

MultiStream is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from minors.

Your rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, export, or delete on our end. For data collected by YouTube, Twitch, or Kick, please refer to their respective policies.

Changes

This policy may be updated. The date at the top of this page indicates the last revision. Changes take effect upon publication.
